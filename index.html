What constitutes a creative web project today?

I believe a creative project can be something that is created without a certain construct whether it is data visualization, animation, photography, etc. Creativity can be endless on the web just as it is in reality.

How do you wish to employ the web or internet as a creative platform?

I want to use my website as a portfoilio to showcase the different creativity aspects that I feel comfortable in whether it is writing or photography.

What are your goals in this course?

I want to push myself to learn things that I consider challenging and complex and become more comfortable with approaching web design.
